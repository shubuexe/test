About Us

Our Mission

The Electronic Circuit Simulation Lab at Shree L.R Tiwari College Of Engineering is dedicated to providing an accessible, interactive platform for students and educators to learn, experiment with, and master electronic circuit design and analysis.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by Shubham Patel and Himanshu Sharma, the Electronic Circuit Simulation Lab began as a final-year project. What started as a tool to help fellow students understand complex circuit behaviors has grown into a comprehensive platform.

Our Approach

We believe in learning by doing. Our simulations allow users to:

  • Experiment with circuit parameters
  • Visualize behaviors with dynamic graphics
  • Advance from basic to complex concepts
  • Test knowledge with challenges

Educational Impact

  • Served over 500 students
  • Adopted by 12 engineering colleges
  • 10,000+ simulations run
  • Recognized by IEEE Student Branch

Our Vision

We aim to expand our simulations and reach students globally with accessible electronics education.
